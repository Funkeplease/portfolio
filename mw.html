Morning Fresh Repositioning Campaign

Brief: Whilst celebrating Morning Fresh at 30, develop a 360 degree campaign that positions Morning Fresh as a carer for the ones who care.

IDEA: She Cares, We Care. Because even caregivers need care, this idea appropriately positions Morning Fresh as a brand that cares for her. The inspiration behind our over 30-year long commitment to quality and effective products is the fact that our Sheroes care for us all in immeasurable ways that deserves recognition. It’s why we soften her tough jobs and support her even beyond dishwashing.

Campaign Framework

Phase 1, Social Experiment
Phase 2, Storydoing
Phase 3, Digital Activation

Social Experiment

We launch the campaign with a social experiment in form of a video interview asking individuals questions that relate to them being cared for and nurtured. Questions like, Who was the first person to ever care for you? Can you remember one time you were sick, who cared for you? When you hear the word Nurturer who pops in your head? Who is the caregiver in your life? When there is an emergency in your home, who do you call? When you need advice, who do you call? ‘’Cares for me, loves me, ensure I am good, provides for me and guides me’’ Who does this for you? Describe your favorite person and who is he/she? Who is your listening ear? Who makes you happy?etc. From this interview we would highlight how many times the word Mom, wife, mother, sister, female friend etc appears in their responses. The aim of this social experiment is to highlight the significance of women as caregivers.
